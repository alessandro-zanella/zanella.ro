About Us

ZANELLA Lounge is a quiet-luxury social space in Cluj-Napoca, designed for those who value connection over chaos. With a warm industrial aesthetic of concrete, wood, plants, and curated light, every detail is intentional — from hosted service to signature cocktails built on local botanicals. Limited Social Suites ensure your night feels exclusive, effortless, and unforgettable.
